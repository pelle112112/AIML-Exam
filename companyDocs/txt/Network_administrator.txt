Title: Network Administrator
Job Description:
As a Network Administrator at PECO Software, you work to ensure network availability, security, and performance. You are part of the operations team and work both with daily operations and larger network projects.
Tasks:
Configuration and maintenance of network equipment (routers, switches, firewalls)
Monitoring network traffic and troubleshooting
Implementing security policies and network segmentation
Documentation and compliance work
Support other teams with network-related issues
Skills:
Deep understanding of TCP/IP, routing, and switching
Experience with firewalls and VPNs
Knowledge of network monitoring tools (e.g., Zabbix, Nagios)
Certifications such as CCNA/CCNP are a plus
Personal Skills:
Analytical and security-conscious
Works systematically and efficiently
Good collaboration skills
Clear and precise communication
