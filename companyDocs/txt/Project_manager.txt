Title: Project Manager

Job Description:

In the role of Project Manager at PECO Software, you will be responsible for planning, coordinating, and executing software projects. You will ensure that projects are delivered on time, within budget, and in accordance with business objectives.

Tasks:

Develop project plans and manage project deliverables
Coordinate between development, design, and business teams
Risk assessment and resource management
Facilitate status meetings and report to management
Monitor and optimize project progress and quality
Skills:

Experience with Agile and traditional project management methodologies
Knowledge of tools such as Jira, Confluence, or MS Project
Understanding of software development lifecycles
Personal Skills:

Excellent communication skills
Structure and overview
Ability to manage multiple stakeholders
Results-oriented and decisive
