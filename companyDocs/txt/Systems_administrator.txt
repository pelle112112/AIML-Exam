Title: Systems Administrator

Job Description:

As a Systems Administrator at PECO Software, you are responsible for ensuring stable operation and security of our IT infrastructure. You will work closely with DevOps and support teams and have a central role in the maintenance of both on-premise and cloud-based environments.

Tasks:

Monitor and maintain servers and networks
Manage user access and security policies
Troubleshooting and resolving system-related incidents
Backup, recovery, and disaster recovery planning
Automation of routine tasks via scripts
Skills:

Experience with Windows and Linux servers
Knowledge of virtualization (VMware, Hyper-V)
Basic networking understanding (TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP)
Experience with cloud platforms (Azure, AWS)
Scripting in PowerShell, Bash, or similar
Personal Skills:

Structured and detail-oriented
High sense of responsibility
Ability to work proactively and solution-oriented
Good collaboration skills across teams
