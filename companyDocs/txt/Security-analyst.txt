Title: Security Analyst

Job Description:

As a Security Analyst at PECO Software, you will protect our systems, data, and employees against threats. You will identify vulnerabilities, recommend improvements, and work with risk management.

Tasks:

Monitor systems for suspicious activity
Perform vulnerability scans and penetration tests
Implement security measures and policies
Educate internal teams in cybersecurity
Participate in incident response and log analysis
Skills:

Knowledge of SIEM, IDS/IPS, and endpoint security
Experience with security standards (ISO 27001, NIST)
Basic understanding of networks and system architecture
Knowledge of scripting for analysis (Python, Bash)
Personal Skills:

Security-conscious and risk-focused
Analytical and detail-oriented
High integrity
Ability to act quickly and effectively
